Staff Development

The staff form the spine of RLHP’s philanthropic interventions. Improving skills and effectiveness of the staff is at the core of the organisation’s work structure. Capacity building sessions are regularly organised on topics of state and central relevance related to health, education, livelihood and SDGs. They are trained in documentation and report writing, designing and executing monitoring and evaluation, leadership and personal development, etc. Monthly review meetings, open discussions and annual staff performance appraisals about scope for improvement are undertaken diligently to reorient all the members with their goals and objectives.
