Nutrition Programme

Nutrition Programme

Main focus of this programme is to provide counselling to the parents of malnourished children regarding the importance of nutritious food.
These counselling sessions are conducted after malnourished kids are identified by a general survey. Nutritious food and its impact on a child's mental physical health is explained in this intervention.
