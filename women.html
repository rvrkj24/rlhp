Women Empowerment

Women are vulnerable when it comes to well being of mental and physical health as a result of the huge burden of work. Women empowerment is achieved when the economic, political, social identity of women is recognised and incorporated into existing systems.
-RLHP has been giving a platform for women to take initiative and solve community level issues through lobbying with the government, monitoring of schools, interventions with social organizations etc. Training and capacity building for women livelihood upliftment are also conducted. -Women federations like DWANI, BELAKU and other groups work together for protecting women from marital abuse and empower their life with dignity, respect and equality
